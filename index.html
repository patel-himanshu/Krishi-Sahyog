OVERVIEW

In todayâ€™s time, often farmers face difficulty in identifying which crops to be grown at what time of the year, resulting in crop failure, which in turn increases the financial burden on them, and thus leads to increasing farmer suicides. This problem can be solved by the proper application of modern technology, and making it accessible to each and every farmer, without creating any sort of disparity among them. This platform does the very same work for farmers. It is an adaptable, user friendly web platform, which would give an insightful analysis, suggesting them the crop selection at a particular time of the year, which would maximize yield by summing up the analysis of various parameters affecting the produce of the crop. It would also guide them to make more rewarding choices for the crop selection by incorporating the use of machine learning. It would help yield healthier crops, reduce workloads and make more profits, thereby helping in the betterment of the financial condition of farmers across the nation, and eliminating the problems pertaining to food shortage.
